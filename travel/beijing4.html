Day 1 | 6月6日 FRI

早抵首都机场 Morning arrival PEK.

酒店寄存行李 Luggage Drop-off at Hotel.

灯市口/王府井附近午餐 Lunch near Dengshikou/Wangfujing. (用餐 Dining: 1h 30m)(推荐 Recommendation: 馅老满 Xiàn Lǎo Mǎn - 饺子 Dumplings / 东来顺 Dōng Lái Shùn - 涮肉 Hot Pot (附近 branch nearby))

王府井大街初步探索 Light exploration Wangfujing. (游玩 Approx. 1h)

14:00 酒店办理入住 Check-in at Hotel.

稍作休息 Brief Rest. (休息 Approx. 1h)

傍晚深入探索王府井 Evening explore Wangfujing. (游玩 Approx. 1.5h+)

Day 2 | 6月7日 SAT

酒店或附近早餐 Breakfast at/near hotel. (用餐 Dining: 40m)

故宫博物院 Forbidden City. (游玩 Approx. 3h)

继续游览故宫 Continue Forbidden City or exit. (游玩 Approx. 1-2h)

步行 Walk to Jingshan Park. (交通 Travel: Approx. 15m)

景山公园 (俯瞰) Jingshan Park (Overlook). (游玩 Approx. 1h)

晚餐 Dinner. (用餐 Dining: 1h 50m)(推荐 Recommendation: 大隐酒食 Dàyǐn Jiǔshí (鼓楼附近 Taiwanese) / 返回灯市口附近 湘满楼 Xiāng Mǎn Lóu - 湘菜 Hunan Cuisine)

Day 3 | 6月8日 SUN

出行 贴士

Travel Tips

  • 提前预订热门景点门票，尤其是故宫和长城。 Book popular tickets (Forbidden City, Great Wall) in advance.
  • 善用便捷的地铁系统，办理交通卡或使用App支付。 Utilize the efficient subway system; get a transport card or use mobile payment.
  • 6月天气较热，注意防晒补水。 June can be hot; use sunscreen and stay hydrated.
  • 准备舒适的鞋子，部分行程步行较多。 Wear comfortable shoes as there's significant walking.
  • 网络与支付：提前准备好网络方案和支付方式。 Network & Payment: Prepare your internet access and payment methods.
  • 用餐高峰需等位，善用在线取号或预订功能。 Expect queues during peak dining hours; use online queuing/booking apps.
